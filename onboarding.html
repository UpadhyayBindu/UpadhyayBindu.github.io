Designing User Onboarding

Team: Bindu, Florence, Leon and Monika Duration: 6 weeks

My Role

  • Literature Review and heuristic evaluation
  • User Interviews
  • Data Analysis (From Google Analytics)
  • Wireframing in Balsamiq
  • Co-creation through card sorting
  • Presentation to the client

Brief

Eventix, a ticketing platform for events, wanted to convert their site visitors to loyal customers. We dove into persuasive principles to design the user onboarding process.

Gathering requirements

  • Interview CEO of Eventix to understand business goals
  • "What is Onboarding?"
  • Interviewed existing and potential customers
  • Outcome: Generated an extensive and prioritised list of requirements

Ideation

The first point of contact for a potential Eventix customer is their website. After gathering the needs, we decided to redesign the website to onboard new users. For this, we used Cialdini's principles of persuasion and personas.

Prototyping and User Testing

We created several modules to reflect the principles of persuasion. For the user testing, we asked the users to build their version of the Eventix website while Thinking Aloud. This was a very interesting method as it enabled us to eliminate some modules. We followed an iterative process to made design changes based on the test results.

Outcome

We presented the outcome of the website design to Eventix. Along with this, we also gave them design guidelines for future use. One of our modules "Engage" was liked very much by them as this enabled them to not only give a quick preview of their product, but also to get in touch with site visitors personally.
